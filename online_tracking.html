Track and Trace

How do I use Track & Trace?

Simply enter the reference number from your documentation or as provided by the sender and click on ‘Track’ Button. The status of your item will then be shown. In most circumstances you will also be able to view an electronic Proof of Delivery, so you can see exactly who signed for your item.

If you need further help, try reading the answers to some of the other questions below.

How do I track an item sent from abroad?

Track and Trace is a SpeedLink Time Critical that lets you check the status of a trackable item that you sent through the post.
